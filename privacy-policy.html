1. Information We Collect

ItineraGeo is designed to be a powerful offline-first tool. This means the core functionality of the app processes data directly on your device, giving you full control. This section breaks down the specific types of data the app accesses to function properly. Click on each item to see the details.

What we collect:

We access your device‚Äôs precise location (latitude, longitude, altitude) via GPS to provide core mapping and sensor functionalities. This includes displaying your current position on a map, providing location coordinates, and using location for georeferencing.

How we use it:

Location data is used in real-time within the app to power features like the map viewer and sensor hub. This data is stored locally on your device as part of your project files in a private SQLite database. We do not transmit this information off your device.

What we collect:

The app uses your device's accelerometer, magnetometer, and gyroscope to power the Geological Compass and other orientation-based features.

How we use it:

This data is processed in real-time on your device to calculate geological measurements like strike, dip, and dip direction. This sensor data is not stored or transmitted.

What we collect:

The app requires access to your device's storage to allow you to import images and map files (like GeoTIFFs) for use in your projects. It also needs storage access to save your projects, corrected images, and exported maps.

How we use it:

All files you import and create are stored in the application's private directory or, when exporting, in a public directory like "Downloads" at your explicit direction. Files are not automatically accessed or transmitted by the app.

What we collect:

To manage premium features, the app uses the Google Play Billing Library. We do not collect, process, or store any of your financial information.

How we use it:

All purchase transactions are handled securely by Google Play. Our app only receives a confirmation token from Google to verify a purchase and unlock the corresponding features.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide and improve the functionality of ItineraGeo. We believe your data is yours, and our use of it is limited to making the app work for you directly on your device.

  • Enable you to create, save, and manage geological mapping projects on your device.
  • Display maps and your location accurately.
  • Provide precise measurements from device sensors.
  • Process images that you select for correction and georeferencing.
  • Unlock premium features upon successful purchase validation.

All your project data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to it, and it is not uploaded to any cloud server automatically.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information or project data to outside parties. Your data is your own. The only exceptions are:

  • With Your Explicit Consent: If you choose to export and share a project file, you are in control of where that data goes.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation.

4. Data Security

We are committed to protecting your data. All project data is stored in a local SQLite database within the application's sandboxed storage on your device, which is protected by the Android operating system's security measures.

5. Data Retention

We do not collect or retain any personal data on our servers. All data you create within the app is stored on your device and remains there until you choose to delete the app or its data.
